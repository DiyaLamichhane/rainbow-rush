## 🎮 HOW TO PLAY

**TAP the falling blocks** before they hit the bottom!

- ✅ **Tap correctly** = +10 points
- ❌ **Miss a block** = -1 life (10 lives total)
- 🔥 **Combo** = Consecutive taps give bonus multipliers
- 📈 **Progressive speed** = Game gets FASTER as your score increases!
